Our Journey

Passion for Perfect Smiles

Founded in 2010, Smile Faces Dental Clinic began with a simple vision — to make top-quality dental care accessible, comfortable, and even enjoyable. Our founder, Dr. Amelia Hartwell, believed that every patient deserves a warm welcome, transparent communication, and treatment that truly transforms lives.

Today, our state-of-the-art facility is home to 25+ specialists using the latest dental technology. We've helped over 10,000 patients achieve the smiles they've always dreamed of, and we're just getting started.
